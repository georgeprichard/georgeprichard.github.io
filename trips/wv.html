West Virginia: New River Gorge

The weekend after I arrived, we went on a trip to New River Gorge, West Viginia with a group of Berry's friends. White water rafting, and deep water soloing: both awesome, especially the latter, which is climbing routes which have water going up half way. You reach the top, and just fall right in. Sadly, no photos of that :(.

This is a bit temporally confusing - I didn't actually have my camera when I went on this trip yet, hence the shitty photos from my phone.

It's interesting, though I wouldn't say the ladders were terribly dangerous, if you did fall you could certainly get a lot of hurt. I get the feeling despite all the legislation you hear about, there's a few examples of things we did in the North America that would probably not be allowed in most of Europe - these ladders, old bridges (later on in Canada), hiking trails forcing you along cliff edges. Kinda fun.
